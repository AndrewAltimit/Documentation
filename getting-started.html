AI in 5 Minutes

What is AI?

Imagine teaching a computer to recognize patterns, just like you learned to recognize letters as a child. AI is about creating computer systems that can:

  • üéØ Learn from examples - Show it many pictures of cats, and it learns what a cat looks like
  • üîÆ Make predictions - Based on weather patterns, predict if it will rain tomorrow
  • üí¨ Understand language - Read this text and answer questions about it
  • üéÆ Make decisions - Choose the best move in a game of chess

Think of AI Like...

A Learning Child

Just as children learn by seeing many examples (this is a dog, that is a cat), AI learns patterns from data.

A Smart Filter

Like a coffee filter separates grounds from liquid, AI can separate spam emails from important ones.

A Digital Assistant

Similar to how a human assistant learns your preferences, AI adapts to help you better over time.
